# Coconut Samui Rugby Academy — Instagram Content Plan

### 3 ready-to-shoot posts · vertical 9:16 for Reels/Stories · @coconutrugby

> Principle: fun-first, zero-pressure. Every piece points to the **free first session** and a
> **WhatsApp** CTA to grab a trial slot. One shoot at a Lamai / Koh's33 session can capture
> everything below in a single afternoon.

---

## 🎬 POST 1 — Reel: "Kids session highlight" (fast cuts)

**Format:** Reel 9:16, 12–20 s, trending upbeat sound.
**Angle:** Show the joy in the first 3 seconds — a parent scrolling should *feel* the energy before they read a word.

**Shot list / edit**
- 0–2 s — Cold open on the biggest moment: a kid touching down + the group cheering. Text overlay: *"First session is free 🏉"*
- 2–8 s — Fast cuts (0.5–1 s each): a coordination/ladder drill, kids laughing, a pass, a scrum-half feeding, a proud high-five.
- 8–14 s — One "hero" moment held slightly longer: the try-line celebration or a group jump.
- 14–18 s — Text overlay: *"Ages 4–10 · Lamai · every week"* over kids walking off smiling.
- End card — Logo/badge + *"First session free — DM or WhatsApp to book."*

**Caption (paste-ready)**
> First session is free — and it might be the best 10 minutes of their week. 🏉😄
> Ages 4–10, all levels, zero pressure. Just fun, teamwork, and a lot of laughing.
> 📍 Lamai / Koh's33 · WhatsApp us for a free trial slot 👉 link in bio
> #cocosamuirugby #kohsamui #kohsamuikids #rugbythailand #kidsactivities

**Secondary hashtags:** #lamai #samuifamily #kidssports #thailandkids #islandlife #rugbylife

**CTA:** Tag @coconutrugby, send "TRIAL" on WhatsApp to book a free slot.

## 🎠 POST 2 — Carousel: "Meet the Coach + program breakdown"

**Format:** Carousel, 5 slides. Reaches parents who scroll slowly rather than watch video.
**Angle:** Build trust with the coach's story, then make it obvious there's a program for every age.

**Slide breakdown**
- **Slide 1 — Meet the Coach.** Photo of Cyril in coaching gear. Text: *"Meet Coach Cyril — 13 years playing & coaching rugby, now on Samui."*
- **Slide 2 — Kids (4–10).** One-line value prop: *"Confidence, coordination and their first taste of teamwork."*
- **Slide 3 — Teens (11–17).** *"Teamwork, skills and real performance — on and off the pitch."*
- **Slide 4 — Adults.** *"Social fitness and a proper team, whatever your level."*
- **Slide 5 — How to start.** Schedule + location (Lamai / Koh's33) + *"First session free — WhatsApp to book."*

**Caption (paste-ready)**
> The island's first structured rugby program — and there's a group for every age. 🏉
> 👦 Kids 4–10 · 🧑 Teens 11–17 · 🧔 Adults
> Coach Cyril brings 13 years of playing and coaching. Come find out where your family fits.
> 📍 Lamai / Koh's33 · First session free 👉 WhatsApp in bio
> #cocosamuirugby #kohsamui #rugbythailand #samuifamily #kohsamuisport

**Secondary hashtags:** #lamai #rugbycoach #youthrugby #teamsport #expatsamui #islandlife

**CTA:** "Which age group is yours?" — reply in comments or WhatsApp for the schedule.

- [ ] Film vertical 9:16; shoot in morning or late-afternoon light.
